Baked Avocado Eggs

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 avocados
  • 4 eggs
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional toppings: chopped herbs, shredded cheese, diced tomatoes, etc.

Instructions:

  1. Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits.
  2. Scoop out some avocado flesh to make room for the eggs.
  3. Crack an egg into each avocado half.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Bake in a preheated oven at 375°F (190°C) for about 15-20 minutes, or until the eggs are set to your liking.
  6. Remove from the oven and top with your favorite toppings.
