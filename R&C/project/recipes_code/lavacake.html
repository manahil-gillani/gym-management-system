Healthy Chocolate Lava Cake

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 10 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons coconut flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 2 tablespoons almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 squares of dark chocolate

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix together coconut flour, cocoa powder, honey or maple syrup, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add almond milk, coconut oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients and mix until well combined.
  4. Divide the batter into two ramekins. Place a square of dark chocolate into the center of each ramekin.
  5. Bake for 8-10 minutes until the edges are set but the center is still soft.
  6. Remove from the oven and let cool for a few minutes before serving.
