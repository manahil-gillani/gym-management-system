3-Ingredient Strawberry Boba

Prep Time: 5 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 20 minutes

Serves: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1/4 cup tapioca pearls
  • 2 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Cook tapioca pearls according to package instructions.
  2. Blend strawberries with water until smooth.
  3. Fill glasses with cooked tapioca pearls and pour strawberry mixture over them.
