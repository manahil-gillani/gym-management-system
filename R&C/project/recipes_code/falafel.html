Falafel Waffles

Prep Time: 15 minutes

Cook Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 1 can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the waffle iron.
  2. In a food processor, combine chickpeas, parsley, garlic, cumin, coriander, baking powder, salt, and pepper. Pulse until finely chopped.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a bowl and stir in flour and olive oil.
  4. Spoon the mixture onto the preheated waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions until golden brown and crispy.
  5. Serve the falafel waffles warm with your favorite toppings.
